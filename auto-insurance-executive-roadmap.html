Phase Details
Phase 1

"Building the Auto Insurance user journey"

Goal: Show users an auto journey that is contextual / relevant offers to drive engagement into the insurance segment. 
Product Marketing Savings-Insight Cards
Discover feed cards and outbound messages showing personalized benchmarks: "Drivers your age in [city] pay $X/mo." The doubt-creating number is the single most important acquisition surface.
Data Smart Targeting Rules
Segment logic: auto loan holders, credit improvers, high-premium states (FL/MI/LA/CA), new users. Only show insurance to users with car-ownership signals.
Product Direct-to-Offers Experience
High-confidence segments skip confirmation entirely. Branded carrier offers shown directly in Discover and in-app surfaces. Minimal friction for users with complete data.
Product Animated Loading Screen Redesign
New animated loading experience designed to handle up to 18 seconds of carrier quote retrieval. Keeps users engaged with progress steps and carrier branding during the wait. In progress; UI attached on PM dashboard.
Product Auto Tab Redesign
Remove redundant PII widget. Replace horizontal scroll with vertical scannable offer list (EWA pattern). Reduce friction for users who have already shown intent.
Marketing Outbound Orchestration
Configure Hightouch + Universal Nurture for insurance cross-sell. Renewal timing, new car purchase, and credit milestone segments. Launch early pilots.
Product Rate Journey Widget
Persistent dashboard widget: estimated range, last checked date, credit improvement callout. Transforms one-time flow into persistent engagement. The anchor of the long-term experience.
Partnership Data Trellis Readiness
Data hydration spec, carrier prefill validation, IC eligibility cohort sizing, Rewards integration discovery. Everything Trellis needs to succeed on day one.
What this unlocks: A warm, insurance-aware audience. Proven targeting and distribution channels. A data hydration contract ready for Trellis. IC business case validated. When Phase 2 arrives, it launches into demand, not a cold start.

Phase 2

"MoneyLion Finds You a Better Rate"

3 through 6 months. Goal: Replace click-out lead-gen with in-app bindable quotes. Turn Phase 1 distribution into a conversion machine.
User belief shift: From "MoneyLion can show me rates" to "MoneyLion switched my insurance in 60 seconds and covered my down payment."
Product Partnership Hollow Flow Integration
Savvy marketplace embedded behind all Phase 1 surfaces. Bindable quotes from 80+ carriers. User confirms 3-5 fields instead of 30-50. All Phase 1 distribution routes to Savvy instead of MediaAlpha.
Data Data Hydration Pipeline
ML first-party signals (bank link, credit trajectory, cash flow, vehicle data, IC eligibility) flow downstream to carriers. Better quotes. Higher CPC/CPQL. Schema defined in Phase 1.
Product IC Down Payment Activation
Instacash advance offered inline at the binding moment. Covers new policy's down payment, bridging the gap before old policy refund arrives. Economics validated in Phase 1.
Data Transaction-Triggered Targeting
Detect renewal approaching and premium increases from bank transaction data. Surface alternatives at the moment of highest frustration. Phase 1 targeting rules expanded with transaction signals.
Product In-App Bind + Auto-Cancel
One-tap policy switch without leaving MoneyLion. Savvy agents handle old policy cancellation. Refund tracked. Licensed agent assist for complex cases.
Product Garage Integration
Vehicle and policy data from Trellis/Savvy stored in Garage. Enables savings prompts, refinancing, monitoring, and cross-product personalization.
Product Rate Journey + CoPilot Upgrade
Both now reference actual premium data from bank transactions: "You pay [Carrier] $221/mo. Based on your profile, you could pay $148." Real numbers, not estimates.
Product Direct-to-Offers Experience
High-confidence segments skip confirmation entirely. Branded carrier offers shown directly in Discover and in-app surfaces. Minimal friction for users with complete data.
What this unlocks: MoneyLion becomes the only fintech where a user goes from "I wonder if I'm overpaying" to "I just switched, ML covered my down payment, and I'll get an alert if my rate goes up" without leaving the app.
> We also want to pay users for auto if we can't find them a better deal, but this needs to be worked on with Tom on datapoints on how we can enable it. 
Phase 3

"MoneyLion Watches Your Insurance For You"

6 through 12 months. Goal: Build features only MoneyLion can build. Make the experience impossible to replicate.
User belief shift: From "MoneyLion saved me money" to "MoneyLion handles my insurance. I don't think about it anymore."
Product Partnership Continuous Rate Monitoring
Trellis re-checks 150+ carriers against the user's profile on schedule and on trigger (credit change, market shift, renewal). Notifies when savings exceed threshold.
Product Insurance Health Score
Coverage adequacy, premium efficiency, and gap assessment displayed alongside credit score on the dashboard. Insurance becomes a daily number that moves when finances move.
Product Financial Stress Protection
Detect cash-flow stress before premium due date from bank data. Offer IC advance to prevent lapse, or recommend temporary coverage adjustment. No competitor has bank data + IC + carrier relationship.
Product Split for Ongoing Premiums
After IC covers the down payment, Split finances ongoing monthly premiums. Recurring revenue for MoneyLion. Recurring touchpoint with the user. No competitor has this.
Partnership Embedded at Car Purchase
Insurance surfaces at auto loan approval through 1,200+ enterprise partner network. Vehicle known, user in buying mindset, quote instant. 72% of buyers want this (87% Gen Z/Millennials).
Partnership Agent-to-MoneyLion Onboarding
Licensed Savvy agents help users sign up for MoneyLion during insurance journey. Insurance acquires ML users instead of ML users discovering insurance. Reverses the funnel.
Data Financial Health Score Signal
ML's proprietary score (credit trajectory + cash flow stability + bill payment consistency) sent to carriers as a lead-quality signal. Improves CPC/CPQL. Not underwriting innovation, matching innovation.
Product Garage as Data Moat
Vehicle, policy, and insurance spending data stored permanently. Enables multi-vehicle management, refinancing prompts, savings tracking. Gets more valuable with every interaction.
The moat: Requires Trellis carrier network + ML financial data + daily app engagement + product ecosystem. No single competitor has all four. Insurance becomes a set-it-and-improve-it background service.

Key Risks

Top Risks and Mitigations

Trellis integration delayed beyond month 6
Critical
Phase 1 is designed to deliver standalone value independently of Trellis. Data hydration spec and carrier validation proceed in parallel regardless. Weekly sync with Trellis team on timeline.
Credit Karma expands Karma Drive beyond Progressive
High
MoneyLion's counter is ecosystem depth, not data breadth. Accelerate Phase 1 distribution to establish the "financial wellness = better rates" narrative before Credit Karma occupies it.
First impression burn at scale
High
Non-negotiable: friction fixes (pre-fill, loading, trust signals) ship before outbound scales. One shot per user. If someone taps, sees a bad experience, and bails, they will not tap again.
IC business case does not validate
Medium
Validate early, fail cheap. If IC economics don't work for insurance, the referral-fee model still works. Ecosystem story shifts to Split and retention-driven LTV.
